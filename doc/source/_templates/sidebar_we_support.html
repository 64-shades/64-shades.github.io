We Support

The Big Issue is a social enterprise that publishes a fortnightly magazine sold on the streets by people experiencing homelessness, marginalisation, and disadvantage. The magazine is a tool for vendors to earn an income, build confidence, and connect with the community. Beyond the magazine, The Big Issue also runs other social enterprises like the Women's Workforce and Community Street Soccer Program to create work opportunities for those in need.

The Cystic Fibrosis Queensland Bookshop stocks thousands of good-condition, second-hand and pre-loved books at very low prices. As part of the social fabric in Nundah for many years now, the bookshop is a true community hub.
